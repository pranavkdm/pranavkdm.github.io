Pranav Kadam

I am a PhD Candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at University of Southern California (USC) advised by Prof. C.-C. Jay Kuo. I am actively involved in research and development of methods for 3D Point Cloud Analysis and Compression. Presently, I am working at Tencent Media Lab on 3D Volumetric Media Compression. Previously, I have worked at Sony and InterDigital on AI-based Point Cloud Compression. My research at USC is related to 3D Point Cloud Perception.
